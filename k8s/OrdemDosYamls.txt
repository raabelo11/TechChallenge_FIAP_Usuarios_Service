Ordem de aplicação recomendada

ConfigMap e Secret primeiro:

kubectl apply -f configmap.yaml
kubectl apply -f secret.yaml

Depois os Deployments + Services + HPAs:

kubectl apply -f jogos-deployment.yaml
kubectl apply -f pagamentos-deployment.yaml
kubectl apply -f usuarios-deployment.yaml

Testar pods, serviços internos e logs:

kubectl get pods
kubectl get services
kubectl logs <nome-do-pod>
kubectl exec -it <nome-do-pod> -- curl localhost:80
kubectl get hpa
kubectl top pods
